Events

  • click(); add a click listener to element(s).
    • $("button").click(function(){
      alert("button clicked"); })
    • $("button").click(function(){
      $(this).css("background", "pink"); })
    • the above changes the button color when clicked. Keyword "this" is containted in
      jQuery $ symbol
  • keypress(); keydown(); keyup();
    • keypress() is the most common
  • on();
    • acts similarly to addEventListener()
    • most common event we'll use. Can take place of keypress and click methods
    • must specify what event we're listening for.
    • click() vs on('click');
    • on('click') is future proof. Applies effects to items that don't exist yet.
